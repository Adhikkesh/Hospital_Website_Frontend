Consultant's Honors list

To highlight and recognize the achievements of consultants, an Honors List is prepared every month.
All the consultants across the group have been requested to send us their outstanding, extraordinary achievements, as per the category mentioned below:

Categories For Honors List:

  • Awards

  • Fellowships

  • Book edited

  • Clinical honours such as an Advisor to the Armed Forces, prestigious organization

  • Academic honours such as appointment as an Examiners

  • Paper publications

These achievements are shared every month with all consultants across the group. Apollo Hospitals, Group believes that this will further motivate and encourage the consultant.
